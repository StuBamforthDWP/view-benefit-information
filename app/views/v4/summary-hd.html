What is transitional protection?

If someone has transitional protection you can issue a Blue Badge without assessment.

Transitional protection applies when an applicant was either:

  • in receipt of the higher rate of the mobility component of DLA before moving to PIP and has requested a mandatory reconsideration (a formal review) of their PIP award by DWP in the last year
  • in receipt of a lifetime or indefinite award of the higher rate of the mobility component of DLA immediately before moving to PIP
